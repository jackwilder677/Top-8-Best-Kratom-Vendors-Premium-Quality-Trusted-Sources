Third-party lab testing represents the single most important factor when evaluating kratom vendors. Reputable vendors invest significantly in comprehensive testing protocols that protect both their reputation and customer safety.

Essential Testing Requirements:

  • Microbiological Screening: Testing for harmful bacteria, yeast, mold, and other pathogens

  • Alkaloid Profiling: Verification of mitragynine and 7-hydroxymitragynine content
